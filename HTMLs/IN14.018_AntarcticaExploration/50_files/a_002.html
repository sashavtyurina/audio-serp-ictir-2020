The hut that was used in Commonwealth Bay from 1911-14 is still there and it is recognized under the Antarctic treaty as a historic site and monument. The interior of the hut, including personal possessions, canned goods and utensils, has been 'preserved' by the cold and the hut has been restored recently to celebrate the centenary of the expedition.
